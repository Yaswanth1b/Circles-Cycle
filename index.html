About

Circle Cycles is a minimalist rhythm and reaction game.

The goal is simple but challenging: hold to expand your circle and release exactly when it aligns with the target ring.

Designed to be a quick, meditative experience, it features dynamic color palettes, procedural audio, and increasing difficulty to test your focus and precision.
